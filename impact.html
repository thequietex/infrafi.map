Energy & Green 190K+ solar nodes, on-chain carbon credits
+
190,000+ Nodes Arkreen's Green Energy Data Nodes track solar output across homes and installations. Each node generates verifiable on-chain Renewable Energy Certificates (RECs).
$100M RWA PowerPod and Arkreen launched a $100M Real World Asset initiative for renewable energy infrastructure across Southeast Asia — EV charging stations and battery-swap cabinets.
50+ Solar Farms Glow protocol incentivizes verified solar farms. The protocol purchases and retires carbon credits proportional to clean energy production — $5M+ annualized revenue.

On-Chain RECs Unlike traditional RECs (opaque, double-counted), blockchain-verified certificates are transparent, immutable, and auditable — solving the greenwashing problem in carbon markets.

The DePIN Revenue Trajectory

From experiment to infrastructure — tracked milestones.

2020
Helium Genesis

Helium deploys first LoRaWAN hotspots. The "mine crypto by providing wireless coverage" model proves people will deploy physical hardware for token rewards.

2021
Storage Wars

Filecoin crosses 1 EiB of storage. Arweave gains traction for NFT metadata permanence. The decentralized storage narrative crystallizes.

2022
DePIN Term Coined

Messari popularizes "DePIN" as a category. Subcategories formalized: Compute, Storage, Wireless, Sensors, Bandwidth, Energy.

2023
Revenue Proof

Total DePIN sector revenue reaches ~$30M. Storage leads with $25M. Hardware costs drop 95%. Helium migrates to Solana for scalability.

2024
VC Floodgates

$850M+ invested in 165+ DePIN startups. io.net hits $1B valuation. Render migrates to Solana. GPU demand explodes with AI boom.

2025
Enterprise Adoption

Market cap hits $19.2B (270% YoY). AT&T partners with XNET. Akash used by NVIDIA-acquired brev.dev. 42.6M total devices.

Jan 2026
$150M Monthly Revenue

DePIN sector hits unprecedented $150M in a single month. Aethir, Render, and Helium lead. The infrastructure layer is no longer speculative — it's generating real recurring revenue at scale.
